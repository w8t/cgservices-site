Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

CG Services ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our gaming analytics software and services.

2. Data Collection

Our software is designed with privacy as a priority. Your gaming session data, performance metrics, and analytics are processed and stored locally on your device.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your gaming session data to our servers.

3. Information We May Collect

We may collect limited information necessary for software operation:

  • License key information for software activation
  • Basic device identifiers for license validation
  • Contact information when you reach out to us

4. Data Storage

All gaming analytics and session data remain on your local device. We do not have access to this data. You maintain full control over your data and can delete it at any time by removing the application data from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Our software may interact with third-party gaming platforms as directed by you. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

6. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information we process. Since gaming data remains on your device, the security of that data depends on your device's security measures.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sales@cgservices.us
